Macaé River, Rio de Janeiro

The Macaé River is a river of Rio de Janeiro state in southeastern Brazil that flows into the Atlantic Ocean at the town of Macaé.
The Macaé River Whatershed comprises about 1,765 km². Our White Water Rafting Tour in Rio de janeiro , is done in the Macaé river , located in the district of Casimiro de Abreu. around 02 hours from Rio de Janeiro city. The river is 80 km (50 miles) long with a rafting course of 8 km (5 miles) long. Once in the river, the rafting experience can last from 1:30 to 2 hours, according to the water level. The course has around 15 rapids, ranging from level 01 to 05 in summer, 01 to 04 in spring, 01 to 03 in fall and 01 to 02 in winter. Usually, in summer we have a higher water level, which increases its difficulty and rapids speed. So in those days, rafting experience is more intense but with a shorter ride. In winter, the ride can be a little longer as water level decreases, as does the speed. The average water temperature is around 22º C (70ºF), year round. The landscape along the rafting course is awesome, and travelers can witness the lush vegetation of the Atlantic Forest.

2 hours (approx.)

Brotas - Jacaré Pepira River, São Paulo

You must already know the traditional Rafting of the Naturally Fun City, but this great news came to shake the life of the most adventurous. Rafting is one of the most popular sports for tourists coming to Brotas. There are thousands of people eager for emotion that recharge their energies and enjoy rafting down one of the best rapids in Brazil. Rafting is performed in the waters of the Jacaré Pepira River, considered one of the best for this sport. The inflatable boat holds up to seven people, and the course of Rio is reward of the nature to the practitioners who raced rapids, ebb and falls. The Rafts Elite Rafting has characteristics very different from the traditional one. If in the raft boat seven people usually participate in the activity, in this new experience everything is different. Here the boat is smaller and narrower, that is, the speed in the rapids is much greater and the stability less! Less stability + speed = much more adventure and contact with the waters of the Jacaré Pepira River.

3 hours (approx.)

Apuama - Cubatão River, Florianópolis

A unique adventure with a lot of adrenaline in one of the main rivers of Greater Florianópolis,connect with nature and with the people you love in this unforgettable activity. The white water Rafting on Cubatão River first leads through slow water before it gets more challenging because of narrow and faster white water. This varied day trip from Florianopolis offers amazing views, a lot of adrenaline and at the end of the adventure an optionally cliff jumping.Escape the crowds of the city and experience Florianóplis's natural landscapes on a white-water rafting adventure on the
Cubatão River. Enjoy a day on the river with friends or family on a white water rafting adventure from Florianópolis.
Our team pick you up at your hotel/pousada in Florianopolis. We arrive at the river in the little village Vargem Grande. The rafting team welcomes you and do a quick briefing. Equipped with life vest, helmet and paddle we get into the raft and paddle down on Cubatao River. On the first kilometer you learn the technical part of how to handle the paddle, because the water is more quiet at the beginning. Soon the last houses are left behind and the mountains and rainforest of Serra do Tabuleiro offer a beautiful view. The rafting gets more challenging as we get down the river, the white water gets narrow and fast. The fast parts are always followed by slower parts, that invites for a bath. After passing numerous falls we arrive at a bay, where we can optionally do a jump from a cliff of 5 meters. This is the last station of our white water rafting adventure. After arriving we will take you back to Florianopolis and to your hotel/pousada.

8 hours (approx.)
